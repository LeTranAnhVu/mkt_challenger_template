About Us

Introduction

Marketing Challengers is a nationwide marketing competition organized by Business Club of RMIT University Vietnam (SGS).

Vision

To become the best student-run marketing competition for students.

Mission

To provide students, regardless of their marketing foundation, a practical playground to develop an Integrated Marketing Communication (IMC) Plan and an opportunity to pitch their ideas to real famous client brands.

Contest

  • Debrief (20/10 - 3/11)

    Opening ceremony: (27/10)

    Workshop 1: Market research + Creative brief (26/10)

  • IMC Plan (3/11 - 17/11)

    Workshop 2: IMC plan + Setting KPI and budget (10/11)

  • Creative production pitching (17/11 - 1/12)

    Workshop 3 (24/11): Creative project training

    Company tour + brief idea (21/10)

    *Back up for company tour (17/10)

    5 teams prepare for the creative project (22-24/10)

    Final showcase - Closing ceremony
