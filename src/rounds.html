IMC plan

1. Timeline: 26/10/2018 - 10/11/2019

2. Content: Candidates will be provided information about Exclusive Sponsor brand and their offerings.

Round 1.1 Market Research

Based on Client Brief, contestants will utilize tools and channels in Market Research to submit a plan of how they conduct secondary and primary research related to the case and the result of their findings (including data and analysis)

Round 1.2 Creative Brief

  • From their conclusion in the market search, contestants will continue creating a Creative Brief which includes these elements:
  • - Client's background
  • - Target audience
  • - Insights
  • - Key message
  • - Big idea

Workshop 1

Workshop 1 about how to conduct effective Market Research and deliver a Creative Brief will be open for all.

3. Content guide line and submission guide line: http://bit.ly/2DfbA1W

4. Submission deadline: 23:59 on 29/9/2019.

5. The results of Round 1: will be announced on 05/10/2019. Only 20 TEAMS with the best results will move to Round 2

  • - Problem of youngsters in Vietnam.
  • - Target Audience insights and background.
  • - Big Idea for the solution that can answer the questions below:
  • Which combination of the above products would you use?
  • Which functions will be apply for the solution?
  • What do the Target Audience need to do in the solution?
  • - Detailed plan for the solution which can meet the
  • T.A needs and wants, using Sponsor’s product as the main tool. The solution needs to satisfy:
  • Which combination of the above products would you use?
  • Which functions will be apply for the solution?
  • What do the Target Audience need to do in the solution?
  • Which key message will be sent to the T.A?

- Value distinction that the solution brings to the T.A (USP). The value needs to be: Helpful, Entertaining, Authentic, Relevant, Timely.

Young people in the age of 18-25 in Vietnam, who are University students, fresh graduates or young workers. They are millennial and gen Z generations, who are early exposed to technology and social media, that makes them eager to learn new things around them. These youngsters are dreamers with purpose and hold an ability to change their world.

Propose a SOLUTION in a form of a BIG IDEA and DEPLOYMENT PLAN to trigger social conversation, inspire youngsters to acknowledge the problem and take action, while using Exclusive Sponsor’s products as the main tools in their marketing campaign. This will ultimately enhance brand consideration.
